About

The appearance of this site was actually from the desire to elaborate with web layout using Twitter Bootstrap and responsive design. That is why it looks so perfect on your iPhone ☺.

But what could be better to write about than Continuous Delivery! Luckily I'm not the only fan of CD. So instead of reinventing the wheel and re-writing the book Continuous Delivery, by Jez Humble and David Farley, I have collected inspirations for the book, as well as presentations and articles from all of the awesome companies that today do CD and share their knowledge.

Warning

Reading the book will make you sad you didn't read it much earlier.
Download:

CD at continuousdelivery.eu

Of course this site practices CD but at a very low scale. Due to it only having static content. I have no build step or unit tests. With a push to master on GitHub.com repo the cloud server is deployed in seconds.☺.
